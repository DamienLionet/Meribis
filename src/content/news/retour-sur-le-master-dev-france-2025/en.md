The [**Master Dev France 2025**](https://www.masterdevfrance.com/) delivered on every promise! This flagship event of the tech community brought together more than **800 developers in competition** and **3,000 enthusiastic visitors**, who came to witness a day defined by performance, innovation and the spirit of sharing.

![](/assets/images/news/retour-sur-le-master-dev-france-2025/inline-01.webp)

Having organised our own online competition [**Code On Time on our new platform**](#jouer_codeontime), it was our turn to rise to the challenge for the fifth consecutive year at this flagship event! Here is a look back at our participation.

## **A high-level technical challenge**

The **Master Dev France** is a live programming contest that puts the best coding talents to the test through challenges in logic, algorithmics and optimisation. For this 9th edition, participants had the choice of **16 programming languages**, including **Python, JavaScript, Java, PHP, C#** and many others.

![](/assets/images/news/retour-sur-le-master-dev-france-2025/inline-02.webp)

The competition took place in several phases:

-   **Hybrid qualifications**: six 45-minute sessions alternating between algorithmics challenges and optimisation exercises to test speed, precision and code efficiency.
-   **Grand Final**: a concluding optimisation challenge pitting the top participants against one another to crown the winners.

This year, a **major new feature** was introduced: the ability for contestants to use **artificial intelligence** as a support tool to solve problems, or to compete solo — placing the confrontation between human ingenuity and emerging technologies at the forefront.

## **A demanding competition and a strong showing for Meritis**

For the fifth consecutive year, Meritis fielded a team of developers ready to take on the challenge. Facing extremely high-level competition, our talents gave their very best throughout the day.
