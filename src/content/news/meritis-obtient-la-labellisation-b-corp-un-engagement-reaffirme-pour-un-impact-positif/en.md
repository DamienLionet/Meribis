<p>Meritis is now <a href="https://bcorporation.fr/" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener nofollow">B Corp™</a> certified! This recognition bears witness to <a href="/en/meritis-csr/">our commitments</a> in the areas of environmental responsibility, social ethics and transparency, whilst reinforcing our determination to combine economic performance with positive impact.</p>

<p>Joining the global B Corp™ movement (short for <em>Benefit Corporation</em>) is a significant milestone that illustrates our ambition to contribute to lasting change in the IT sector. This certification is the result of a collective commitment carried by more than 900 employees, who work every day to make Meritis a responsible and engaged company.</p>

<div style="height:20px" aria-hidden="true" class="wp-block-spacer"></div>

<h2 class="wp-block-heading" id="h-faire-partie-de-la-communaute-b-corp-une-direction-naturelle-pour-meritis">Becoming part of the B Corp community — a natural direction for Meritis.</h2>

<p>At Meritis, we are firmly convinced that economic performance must not come at the expense of social and environmental concerns. In a world where these issues are multiplying, we have chosen to pursue B Corp certification to affirm our determination to contribute to a more sustainable and equitable economy. This certification represents <strong>far more than a label</strong>: it reflects our commitment to <strong>creating value</strong> for <strong>all of our stakeholders</strong> — employees, clients, partners, and society as a whole.</p>

<p>Our choice to pursue B Corp certification is rooted in a long-term vision in which the company&rsquo;s performance is measured not only by its profitability, but also by its capacity to generate lasting positive impact. We believe in growth that respects both people and the environment, and we wish to play an active role in building a more virtuous and viable economic model for the long term. By joining this movement, Meritis commits to placing collective benefit at the heart of its development, in order to reconcile economic success with positive societal contribution.</p>

<p>This determination <strong>to place Meritis within a virtuous system</strong> is in keeping with the <a href="/en/our-dna/"> values</a> that have guided us since our founding: benevolence, closeness, rigour and humility.</p>

<h2 class="wp-block-heading" id="h-un-label-exigeant-et-une-reconnaissance-de-nos-engagements">A demanding label and a recognition of our commitments</h2>

<p>Founded in 2006, the B Corp label aims to bring together a community of companies that integrate <strong>social</strong> and <strong>environmental</strong> considerations into their business model, taking into account the <strong>interdependence</strong> of all stakeholders.</p>

<p>Awarded by the independent non-profit organisation <a href="https://www.bcorporation.net/en-us/movement/about-b-lab/" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener nofollow">B Lab™</a>, the B Corp™ label is recognised as one of the most demanding in the world. It is based on an in-depth assessment of organisations&rsquo; impacts across <strong>five key areas</strong>: governance, environmental stewardship, employee policies, client relations, and community engagement. &nbsp;</p>

<div class="wp-block-image">
<figure class="aligncenter size-full is-resized"><img width="613" height="595" src="/assets/images/news/meritis-obtient-la-labellisation-b-corp-un-engagement-reaffirme-pour-un-impact-positif/inline-03.webp" alt="Meritis - B Corp Areas" class="wp-image-53142" style="width:550px" /></figure>
</div>

<p>In addition to meeting high standards in terms of social and environmental impact, obtaining B Corp™ certification means having made a legal commitment to stakeholder governance and having demonstrated transparency by making one&rsquo;s <a href="https://www.bcorporation.net/en-us/find-a-b-corp/company/meritis/" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener">B Corp performance report</a> publicly available.</p>

<p>Joining the community of <strong>9,480 B Corp™-certified companies</strong> around the world, including 1,248 in France, reinforces our conviction that performance and positive impact can go hand in hand. This certification illustrates our commitment to ethical and responsible practices and our desire to participate in a positive transformation of the IT sector.</p>

<div style="height:20px" aria-hidden="true" class="wp-block-spacer"></div>

<h2 class="wp-block-heading" id="h-une-demarche-d-amelioration-continue">A commitment to continuous improvement</h2>

<p>With a score of 89.5 points, well above the required threshold of 80, Meritis intends to go further by strengthening its commitments in the future. </p>

<figure class="wp-block-image size-large"><a href="https://www.bcorporation.net/en-us/find-a-b-corp/company/meritis/" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"><img width="1024" height="319" src="/assets/images/news/meritis-obtient-la-labellisation-b-corp-un-engagement-reaffirme-pour-un-impact-positif/inline-04.webp" alt="Overall B Impact Score - Meritis 2025 - B Corp certified 89.5" class="wp-image-53161" /></a></figure>

<p>Indeed, obtaining the B Corp™ label is not an end in itself, but rather an additional step in our continuous improvement journey. We have already identified areas for progress in order to strengthen our social and environmental actions. Since its founding, Meritis has placed people at the heart of its strategy, firmly convinced that achievements and developments are above all driven by individuals.</p>

<blockquote class="wp-block-quote is-layout-flow wp-block-quote-is-layout-flow">
<p><em>« Meritis is joining the highly exclusive club of impact-driven companies. Far more than a recognition, this certification bears witness to the determination of the group and its 900 employees — who give it life every day — to generate positive outcomes for society and the environment. Better still, it commits us to continuing our efforts in a process of continuous improvement of our practices. We have already identified areas for progress, in particular by strengthening our social and environmental actions. From its very founding, Meritis has placed people at the heart of its strategy. People must be central to our concerns, as they are the source of all creation and all progress »</em>, explains Eric Varache, Human Resources Director of the Meritis Group.</p>
</blockquote>

<div style="height:20px" aria-hidden="true" class="wp-block-spacer"></div>

<h2 class="wp-block-heading" id="h-un-engagement-porte-par-et-pour-l-humain">A commitment driven by and for people</h2>

<p>At Meritis, we are convinced that technology must serve people, and not the other way around. B Corp™ certification fits squarely within our approach of building a company that is useful to society and respectful of the environment. We will continue to mobilise our teams around concrete initiatives such as skills-based sponsorship, reducing our carbon footprint, and promoting diversity and inclusion.</p>

<blockquote class="wp-block-quote is-layout-flow wp-block-quote-is-layout-flow">
<p>&nbsp;<em>« B Corp™ certification is the reflection of a collective effort and a marker of commitment from everyone within the group. It is a reward, a source of satisfaction, an incentive to maintain the efforts necessary for success, an inspiration for all of our stakeholders, and confirmation of our position as a responsible actor »</em>, concludes <strong>Eric VARACHE</strong>.</p>
</blockquote>
