<p><strong>At Meritis, a B Corp™-certified company, we firmly believe that sport extends far beyond competition: it is a remarkable vehicle for shared values, team cohesion, inspiration and resilience. In that spirit, we are proud and delighted to announce a new partnership with <a href="https://www.instagram.com/baptiste.chassagne/" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener nofollow">Baptiste Chassagne</a>, an elite trail-running athlete — a demanding and authentic discipline that mirrors the commitments we uphold.&nbsp;</strong></p>

<h2 class="wp-block-heading" id="h-des-valeurs-communes-nbsp"><strong>Shared values</strong>&nbsp;</h2>

<p>Our partnership is first and foremost <strong>human and sincere</strong>, built on open-hearted exchanges and a mutual desire to grow together. Baptiste's journey — demanding, inspiring, and deeply rooted in respect for the natural world — resonates with our own corporate culture: one of commitment, excellence, and pushing beyond one's limits.&nbsp;</p>

<p>Baptiste embodies a vision of sport in which performance goes hand in hand with ethics, humility and respect for life. It is precisely this approach that won us over and convinced us to build a lasting, enriching venture together — for our employees as much as for him.&nbsp;</p>

<h2 class="wp-block-heading" id="h-des-temps-forts-pour-inspirer-et-transmettre-nbsp"><strong>Highlights designed to inspire and share</strong>&nbsp;</h2>

<p>This partnership will come to life through several major annual events, conceived as privileged moments for our teams. Workshops, conferences, sporting sessions or opportunities for dialogue: these gatherings will be occasions to share the lessons of elite sport and to bring our values of commitment, solidarity and perseverance to life.&nbsp;</p>

<p>These moments will also give Baptiste the opportunity to pass on his experience: his approach to mindset, effort management and teamwork — all cross-functional skills that are invaluable in the professional world.&nbsp;</p>

<h2 class="wp-block-heading" id="h-une-aventure-qui-s-inscrit-dans-notre-adn-nbsp"><strong>A venture rooted in our DNA</strong>&nbsp;</h2>

<p>This partnership continues our long-standing commitment to sport and its many virtues, already demonstrated through our support of Sébastien Rogues and Sébastien Camus, and through the <strong><a href="https://www.epouvant-trail.com/qui-sommes-nous/" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener nofollow">creation of the Épouvant Trail</a>,</strong> a nature race organised with and for our employees.&nbsp;</p>

<p>By joining forces with Baptiste Chassagne, we reaffirm our commitment to promoting sport that carries meaning — a driver of individual and collective well-being, and a reflection of our societal engagement as a <a href="/en/meritis-csr/"><strong>responsible company</strong></a>.&nbsp;</p>

<p>We thank Baptiste for his trust. We look forward to writing the first pages of this new chapter together — full of emotion, shared experiences and human adventure.&nbsp;</p>
