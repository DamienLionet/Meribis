<p><strong><em>Meritis continues its strategic expansion with the acquisition of <a href="https://neofin.co.uk/" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener">Neofin Advisory</a>, a consulting firm recognised for its expertise in risk management, banking regulation, compliance and sustainable finance.</em></strong></p>

<figure class="wp-block-image size-large"><img width="1024" height="576" src="/assets/images/news/acquisition-neofin-advisory-meritis/inline-01.webp" alt="Neofin Advisory logo" class="wp-image-55443" /></figure>

<h2 class="wp-block-heading" id="h-une-nouvelle-etape-strategique-pour-meritis">A new strategic milestone for Meritis</h2>

<p>Following the successful integration of Navigacom in 2024, this latest transaction is fully aligned with our growth strategy, both at sector and geographical level. It reflects a clear ambition: to strengthen our positioning in financial services whilst consolidating our international presence, with a new strategic foothold in London, the world's financial capital.</p>

<h2 class="wp-block-heading" id="h-une-expertise-metier-enrichie">An enriched domain expertise</h2>

<p>Through this acquisition, Meritis significantly broadens its scope of intervention. Historically positioned alongside the CIOs of major banking institutions, the Group is now able to engage directly with business lines: risk, compliance, ESG, sustainable finance, and trading floors. This positioning enables us to offer our clients end-to-end support, from strategy through to technical implementation.</p>

<h2 class="wp-block-heading" id="h-neofin-advisory-un-acteur-de-reference-base-a-londres">Neofin Advisory, a leading firm based in London</h2>

<p>Founded and headquartered in London, with offices in Paris and Lisbon, <strong>Neofin Advisory</strong> comprises around twenty consultants drawn from leading firms (McKinsey, Deloitte, KPMG…). Over five years, Neofin Advisory has delivered more than 40 projects across 10 European countries for major clients such as BNP Paribas, NatWest and Alantra. It holds a solid portfolio of multi-year contracts, some running through to 2032.</p>

<h2 class="wp-block-heading" id="h-une-vision-commune-pour-anticiper-les-transformations-du-secteur-financier"><strong>A shared vision for anticipating the transformations of the financial sector</strong></h2>

<p>This coming together is built on a shared vision: common standards of excellence, a results-driven culture, and a mutual commitment to anticipating the major transformations of the financial sector, particularly on regulatory and climate-related matters.</p>

<h2 class="wp-block-heading" id="h-une-dynamique-europeenne-en-marche">A European momentum underway</h2>

<p>With this new base in London, alongside Paris, Lisbon and Brussels, Meritis firmly establishes itself within a European development logic, with a strengthened continental presence and a network of active contracts across several countries.</p>

<p><strong>We extend a warm welcome to the Neofin teams, and look forward to building the next chapter of our collective journey together!</strong></p>

<p>The Meritis Group now supports its Corporate and Investment Banking (CIB) clients across both their business challenges and their IT requirements.</p>
