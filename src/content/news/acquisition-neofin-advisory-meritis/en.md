***Meritis continues its strategic expansion with the acquisition of [Neofin Advisory](https://neofin.co.uk/), a consulting firm recognised for its expertise in risk management, banking regulation, compliance and sustainable finance.***

![Neofin Advisory logo](/assets/images/news/acquisition-neofin-advisory-meritis/inline-01.webp)

## A new strategic milestone for Meritis

Following the successful integration of Navigacom in 2024, this latest transaction is fully aligned with our growth strategy, both at sector and geographical level. It reflects a clear ambition: to strengthen our positioning in financial services whilst consolidating our international presence, with a new strategic foothold in London, the world's financial capital.

## An enriched domain expertise

Through this acquisition, Meritis significantly broadens its scope of intervention. Historically positioned alongside the CIOs of major banking institutions, the Group is now able to engage directly with business lines: risk, compliance, ESG, sustainable finance, and trading floors. This positioning enables us to offer our clients end-to-end support, from strategy through to technical implementation.

![photo of Sébastien Videment CEO of Meritis](/assets/images/news/acquisition-neofin-advisory-meritis/inline-02.webp)

> "This coming together is far more than an acquisition: it is a convergence. Our locations complement one another, as do our areas of expertise. And it is through finance that our European development will accelerate."  
> — *Sébastien Videment, founder of Meritis*

## Neofin Advisory, a leading firm based in London

Founded and headquartered in London, with offices in Paris and Lisbon, **Neofin Advisory** comprises around twenty consultants drawn from leading firms (McKinsey, Deloitte, KPMG…). Over five years, Neofin Advisory has delivered more than 40 projects across 10 European countries for major clients such as BNP Paribas, NatWest and Alantra. It holds a solid portfolio of multi-year contracts, some running through to 2032.

![Photo of Alain Amador dos Santos Meritis and Neofin](/assets/images/news/acquisition-neofin-advisory-meritis/inline-03.webp)

> "To continue growing in a demanding environment, we needed a strong group that shared our human values and our professional standards. Meritis was the obvious choice."  
> — *Alain Amador dos Santos, Chairman of Neofin Advisory*

## **A shared vision for anticipating the transformations of the financial sector**

This coming together is built on a shared vision: common standards of excellence, a results-driven culture, and a mutual commitment to anticipating the major transformations of the financial sector, particularly on regulatory and climate-related matters.

## A European momentum underway

With this new base in London, alongside Paris, Lisbon and Brussels, Meritis firmly establishes itself within a European development logic, with a strengthened continental presence and a network of active contracts across several countries.

**We extend a warm welcome to the Neofin teams, and look forward to building the next chapter of our collective journey together!**

The Meritis Group now supports its Corporate and Investment Banking (CIB) clients across both their business challenges and their IT requirements.
