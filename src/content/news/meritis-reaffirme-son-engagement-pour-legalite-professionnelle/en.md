In a technology sector that remains largely male-dominated, ensuring professional equality between women and men remains a major challenge. At Meritis, we make this ambition a daily priority. For 2024, our commitment is once again reflected in a high score of **94/100** on the Gender Equality Index across all our entities, confirming our sustained efforts in favour of gender balance and inclusion.

### A high index score reflecting a committed policy

The Gender Equality Index is issued following a questionnaire mandated by the Ministry of Labour for companies with more than 50 employees. It evaluates the measures implemented against several key criteria.

![Meritis - Gender Equality Index 2024: 94/100](/assets/images/news/meritis-reaffirme-son-engagement-pour-legalite-professionnelle/inline-01.webp)

The results for the Meritis UES (comprising Meritis Holding, Meritis, Meritis Technologies, Meritis Régions and Meritis Lab) for 2024 are as follows:

-   **Pay gap**: 39/40
-   **Pay-rise rate gap**: 20/20
-   **Promotion rate gap**: 15/15
-   **Return from maternity leave**: 15/15
-   **Parity among highest earners**: 5/10

These figures attest to the progress made and the ongoing efforts to guarantee a fair professional environment for everyone.

### Concrete actions to strengthen gender balance

At Meritis, we do not stop at figures: we put tangible initiatives in place to promote diversity and inclusion across our business areas.

-   **Pay transparency and equity**: We have introduced clear salary grids to ensure a fair and consistent remuneration policy, both at the point of hire and during annual reviews.
-   **Promoting women in tech**: We regularly organise events, conferences and after-work gatherings to provide a space for women in the sector to express themselves and exchange ideas.
-   **"Women in Tech" programme**: We give our female colleagues a platform to share their journeys and inspire the next generation of female talent.
-   **Awareness-raising initiatives and training**: We offer tailored workshops and sessions to support our employees in their professional and personal development.

**Samia SEKKAT, Product Owner consultant at Meritis since 2019**

### A commitment that continues into 2025

At Meritis, we remain faithful to our commitments and continue our efforts towards a more inclusive and equitable professional world. We will keep putting concrete actions in place to promote gender balance, raise awareness among our employees, and encourage women's access to careers in tech.

This commitment forms part of an ambitious CSR approach, illustrated by the award of B Corp™ certification at the beginning of 2025. This recognition reflects our determination to build a more responsible business model, fully integrating social, environmental and ethical dimensions at the heart of our strategy. It marks a new milestone in our pursuit of continuous improvement, where economic performance and positive impact go hand in hand.

Our ambition is to make Meritis a benchmark organisation in workplace equality, by putting concrete, measurable solutions in place to promote parity and inclusion.
