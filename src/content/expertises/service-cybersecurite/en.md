## Meritis is fully committed to the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

![Meritis is GDPR compliant](/assets/images/expertises/service-cybersecurite/inline-8.webp)

The European regulation of 23 May 2018, which enshrines the obligations of the GDPR, aims to protect the personal data of users. All European businesses are **legally required to comply** with these consumer protection measures, or face sanctions.
Supervisory authorities allowed a two-year transition period for organisations to achieve compliance. However, some companies are still behind. France alone has already seen **€300 million in fines imposed** in fewer than three years.
