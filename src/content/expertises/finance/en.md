## Our IT and Business Expertise in Capital Markets Finance

![paris la défense ](/assets/images/expertises/finance/inline-1.webp)

**Technology** is now **pervasive across banking information systems**. Over the past decade, the digitalisation of banking departments has become a pivotal focus for the major players in financial services.

To meet the evolving demands of their clients and an ever-growing body of regulatory requirements, large financial institutions have no alternative but to invest heavily in their IT infrastructure.

Meritis places its **teams of functional and technical experts** at the disposal of its clients to help them rise to these challenges.

## The Challenges in Capital Markets Finance

Meritis operates across the entire Front-to-Back Office value chain, from business engineering (Risk / Quantitative) through to the development of applications for business teams (traders, sales, middle office, etc.).

Regulatory and environmental imperatives demand constant evolution in the practices of investment banks, asset managers, and insurers.

Meritis makes its teams of functional and technical experts available to clients including investment banks, asset managers, depositaries, and exchanges.
