## Build and deliver your fully bespoke web platform — resilient, scalable, and secure

When faced with challenges around performance, security, scalability, maintenance, or business alignment, identifying the right solution is rarely straightforward. As experts in bespoke software development, **Meritis designs, builds, and delivers your web platforms.** Meritis also takes care of corrective and application maintenance for the web platforms it brings into service.

### Whatever your needs, Meritis supports you through:

-   **Web platform delivery** – Meritis builds your web platform, dashboard, or intranet, covering wireframe design, cloud deployment, and application maintenance
-   **Project industrialisation** – Meritis helps you industrialise a proof of concept and transform it into a Minimum Viable Product (MVP), establishing a resilient and secure architecture, comprehensive monitoring, and centralised application logging

### Meritis Cloud at your service

Meritis also offers its clients support throughout the deployment of their application solution in the cloud, whether on Azure or AWS.

## Our methodology

**Meritis supports you in implementing the methods best suited to your needs within your teams** through:

-   **UX/UI WIREFRAMING**: At the outset of the project, our UX/UI designers present wireframes that give concrete form to your requirements and make it easy to visualise the finished product.
-   **BUILD & AGILITY**: During the build phase, a dedicated agile team is assigned to your project. Multidisciplinary, technically expert, and self-sufficient in its operation, the team is focused on delivery and quality, within agreed timelines.
-   **INFRA & DevSecOps**: The infrastructure we put in place — in particular our DevSecOps software factory — ensures maximum productivity and security through automated testing and continuous deployment.
-   **MAINTENANCE**: Once the application product has been deployed and is live, our support team provides ongoing application and corrective maintenance.

## Our Craftsmanship culture

Since the late 2000s, the Software Craftsmanship manifesto has redefined what it means to be a developer. It builds on the Agile manifesto, bringing the same flexibility and rigour to the writing and design of code as to the development cycle itself.

For us, the Craftsmanship approach creates a virtuous circle of best practices and continuous skills development that strengthens time-to-market, product quality, and ultimately client satisfaction. Our developers become software craftspeople — in permanent learning mode and supported by a community that helps them grow through training sessions, code quality workshops, and structured exercises, so they can embed best practices into their everyday work (Git, Docker, TDD, and more).

### AGILE METHODOLOGY

**Develop the agility that works for you and optimise your Time To Market!**

**Applying agile principles means giving yourself the means to deliver a solution quickly to your users and to keep improving it continuously.** Far more than a short-term profitability imperative, agility must combine continuous adaptation (to constraints and reality) with forward thinking (creativity and user understanding).

### DEVOPS EXPERTISE

**Deploy DevOps methods to automate, integrate, and deploy continuously!**

**The DevOps approach** sits today **at the heart of enterprise software development practices**, as it represents the **strongest guarantee of project success** in a world where application security and availability are more critical to users than ever before.
