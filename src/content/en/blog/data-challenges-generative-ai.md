Generative AI is reaching every business function. But without reliable, governed data, the promises quickly hit reality.

## Quality over volume

A model is only as good as the data it is given. Before industrialising a use case, you must ensure the **freshness**, completeness and traceability of your sources.

## Governance and compliance

Who accesses what, and for what purpose? Generative AI makes the question unavoidable: GDPR, intellectual property and confidentiality must be framed from the start.

## Securing usage

Data leaks through prompts, hallucinations, bias: guardrails (human review, filtering, logging) are essential for a trustworthy rollout.
