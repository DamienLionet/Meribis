Lean is not just a factory method. Applied to IT, it helps teams focus on what truly creates value.

## Identify waste

Waiting, rework, unnecessary features: lean hunts the **waste** that slows delivery without benefiting the user.

## Smooth the flow

Limiting work in progress, visualising the flow (Kanban) and reducing wait times allow more regular delivery.

## Continuous improvement

Lean relies on **kaizen**: small, frequent improvements driven by the team itself, rather than large reorganisations.
