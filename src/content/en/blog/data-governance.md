Any ambitious data project relies on trustworthy data. Governance is not a bureaucratic layer: it is what makes data genuinely usable.

## Map the sources

Identify where data comes from, who produces it and who consumes it. Without a map, there is no governance.

## Define ownership

Every dataset must have an **owner** responsible for its quality and lifecycle.

## Tool up gradually

Catalogue, quality, lineage: deploy in iterations, starting with the highest-value domains.
