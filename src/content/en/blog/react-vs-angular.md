React and Angular dominate front-end development. Both are mature and backed by major players — but they are not alike.

## Two philosophies

**React** is a library, flexible and minimalist, to be complemented by an ecosystem. **Angular** is a complete, structured and opinionated framework.

## Respective strengths

React shines through its flexibility and community; Angular through its consistency and integrated tooling, valued on large enterprise projects.

## How to choose

The right choice depends on the team, scope and lifespan of the project, more than on any absolute technical superiority.
