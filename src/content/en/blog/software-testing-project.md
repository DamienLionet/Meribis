Software without a test strategy is a risky bet. Quality is built throughout the cycle, not at the end.

## The test pyramid

Many fast **unit** tests, fewer **integration** tests, even fewer **end-to-end** tests: the balance ensures speed and reliability.

## Automate

Integrated into CI, automated tests detect regressions immediately and secure every release.

## Measure the right thing

Coverage is an indicator, not a goal. Better to test critical paths than to chase a percentage at all costs.
