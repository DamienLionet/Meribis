Financial risk is inherent to any market activity. Understanding it well is already starting to master it.

## The main families

**Market**, **credit**, **liquidity** and **operational** risk: each type calls for specific indicators and countermeasures.

## Measuring

VaR, stress tests, scenarios: measurement quantifies exposure and informs allocation and hedging decisions.

## Managing

Diversification, limits, hedging and internal control: risk management combines quantitative tools and governance.
