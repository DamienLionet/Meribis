<h2 class="wp-block-heading has-text-align-left has-black-color has-light-pink-background-color has-text-color has-background" id="h-our-labels-and-awards">Our labels and awards</h2>

<div class="wp-block-columns is-layout-flex wp-container-core-columns-is-layout-9d6595d7 wp-block-columns-is-layout-flex">
<div class="wp-block-column is-layout-flow wp-block-column-is-layout-flow" style="flex-basis:66.66%">
<h3 class="wp-block-heading" id="h-b-corp">B Corp™</h3>

<p><strong>Meritis Earns B Corp™ Certification</strong><br>Meritis is proud to announce its recent B Corp™ certification with a score of 89.5, placing the company among the few organizations in its category to achieve this prestigious recognition. This distinction reflects its commitment to making a positive impact on the environment, society, and its employees.</p>

<p><strong>A Rigorous International Recognition</strong><br>The B Corp™ certification, awarded by the independent organization B Lab™, assesses companies based on five key areas: governance, environmental impact, social policies, customer relationships, and community engagement. With a performance well above the required threshold of 80 points, Meritis demonstrates its dedication to combining economic growth with social responsibility.</p>

<p><strong>A Business Model Serving People and the Planet</strong><br>Since its founding, Meritis has placed people at the heart of its strategy and integrated ethical practices into its IT activities. This certification marks a key milestone in its ambition to continue growing while contributing to concrete initiatives such as skills-based volunteering, promoting diversity, and reducing its ecological footprint.</p>

<p><strong>Meritis Joins the B Corp Community</strong><br>By joining the 1,250 certified companies in France and over 9,480 worldwide, Meritis becomes part of a global movement dedicated to building a more inclusive and sustainable economy.</p>

<p>Meritis thanks its 900 employees for their commitment to this collective project and continues to strive for excellence toward a more responsible future.</p>

<h3 class="wp-block-heading" id="h-ecovadis">Ecovadis </h3>

<p>Each year, Meritis participates in the Ecovadis questionnaire, an independent agency that offers a service that evaluates companies’ CSR performance.</p>

<p>The Ecovadis rating is based on 4 themes:</p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>The environment</li>

<li>Work and human rights</li>

<li>Ethics</li>

<li>Sustainable procurement</li>
</ul>

<p>With an overall score of 71/100, Meritis ranks among the top 5% of companies rated by Ecovadis! By putting people at the center of all its actions, Meritis is committed to providing its employees with a caring and respectful working environment.</p>
