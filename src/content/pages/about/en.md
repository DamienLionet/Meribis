At Meritis, we help organisations secure their technology transformations in complex or critical environments. Our role is to connect strategic vision, architecture and field execution in order to deliver concrete results.

By combining the technical expertise of our consultants with a precise understanding of client challenges, we build reliable, maintainable solutions that create value.

## IT Consulting, Engineering And Delivery

Major IT transformations require a clear trajectory, solid architecture and controlled execution. The Meritis Group supports executive, IT and business departments in defining, structuring and implementing their technology projects across sectors, with reinforced expertise in finance and CIB.

We typically intervene when:

- technology investments are structurally important;
- environments are complex, critical or highly regulated;
- the IT trajectory lacks clarity;
- there is a high risk of disconnect between vision and execution;
- performance, security and reliability requirements are strong.

## A Structured Model

The group relies on three complementary entities, with unified governance and a coherent point of contact depending on each assignment's needs.

### Meritis

As the group's parent company, Meritis carries IT engineering and delivery: industrialisation, application development, modernisation, scaling and operational execution.

### Navigacom

Navigacom operates as an IT strategy consulting firm: technology roadmaps, structuring decisions, governance, IT service performance and CIO support.

### Neofin Advisory

Neofin Advisory is dedicated to finance and CIB transformation consulting: financial and non-financial risks, regulatory compliance, sustainable finance, operational performance and ESG challenges.

## Why IT Departments Choose Us
