There is no universal data architecture. The right choice depends on usage, volume and the maturity of the organisation.

## Warehouse and lake

The **data warehouse** excels at structured analytics; the **data lake** accepts any raw data, at the cost of more demanding governance.

## The lakehouse

The lakehouse aims to combine the best of both: the flexibility of the lake and the transactional rigour of the warehouse.

## The data mesh

An organisational approach, the data mesh distributes data ownership to business domains. Powerful at scale, demanding in governance.
